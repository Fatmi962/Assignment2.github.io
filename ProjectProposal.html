Project Proposal:

Brix Inventory Management System:

The purpose of this project is to build a prototype inventory management system to ease up the manual process.

The manual system employed by organizations involves the physical movement of their audit staff from office to office to manually count the assets residing in each office before entering them into the spreadsheet. This method wastes time is characterized by errors and it is difficult to keep inventory records.

Functional requirements include adding and managing staff and customers, adding and managing products and their categories; furthermore, generating profit/loss reports, adding staff details and generating invoices.
